Want to learn more?

How is Auctologue different from AI description generators?

Auctologue is designed for identification, not just prose generation. It uses research-backed, database-supported identification to produce more reliable listing outputs.

What types of lots can Auctologue identify effectively?

Auctologue is built for mixed-category inventory commonly seen in auctions, resale, estate sales, and other secondhand-heavy operations.

Does the workflow support photos-only cataloging?

Yes. Teams can upload photos and process lots without requiring manual keyword prompts for each item.

How does image processing work for paying customers?

Paying plans include automated image processing options such as background removal, crop/resize handling, and standardized output formatting.

How fast can high-volume catalog teams process lots?

The workflow is engineered for high-throughput operations and can process thousands of lots per hour depending on intake and configuration.

Who can access our catalog data and uploaded files?

Access is restricted to authorized users and required processing services according to the platform’s privacy policy and service controls.

How do we report errors or request product features?

Where can we start using Auctologue right now?
